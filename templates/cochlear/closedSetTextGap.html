Closed Set Training

You are about to start a closed set training module. In this module, you will listen to an unknown sound. This unknown sound could be speech, such as a phoneme, or it could be an environmental sound, such as a river or car horn. Once you have listened to the sound, select the button labeled with what you think the unknown sound is.
